Hotkeys

  • Open My Notes Ctrl + Shift + M (set in chrome://extensions/shortcuts)
  • Open Options Ctrl + Shift + O
  • Toggle Focus mode Ctrl + Shift + F
  • Toggle Sidebar Ctrl + S
  • Toggle Toolbar Ctrl + E
  • Go to Previous note Ctrl + [
  • Go to Next note Ctrl + ]
In OSX, you can also use Cmd instead of Ctrl.

Options

Hides the note title and toolbar, to get more editing space.
Can toggle with "Toggle Focus mode" keyboard shortcut.
My Notes will require a permission to enable this feature.
Stores the notes to your Google Drive in the folder My Notes (created automatically, once), and synchronizes the changes in both ways — to Google Drive, from Google Drive — on every My Notes Open/Close/Refresh, or with the "Sync Now" button.

This gives you:
  • backup (notes can be restored in future)
  • can modify the notes in both sources (Google Drive, My Notes, and vice versa)
  • can modify the notes from other computers (by installing My Notes and using the same Google Account)
My Notes can only access the files it created. It cannot see the other files in your Google Drive.
My Notes will require a permission to enable this feature.
